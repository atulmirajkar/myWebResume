About Me

I have served as a software developer for two years (Aug 2011 - Aug 2013) in Geometric Limited, Pune. Some of the problems being continuously tackled in IT industry and most of which I have encountered in my extensive stint as a software developer at Geometric Limited are related to that of computation of exponentially increasing data. Research programs in the fields of Networking, Distributed computing and that of Algorithm analysis are continuously being carried out to tackle the ever increasing need of efficiency in computation speed and time. With this need in mind my future goal is to pursue a career in IT industry and aim towards computation on big data.

I hold strong expertise in C++ and Java. I am currently a Teaching Assistant for the course “Principles of Programming Languages” at University of Maryland, Baltimore County. For the first semester of my Master’s degree I had taken up the following courses - Design and Analysis of Algorithms, Artificial Intelligence and Natural Language Processing. In the current semester I have taken up Machine Learning, Computer Architecture and Semantic Web. I am interested in applying Machine learning and Natural Language Processing techniques to process data.
